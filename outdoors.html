This week's hidden gem is the gorgeous Foscott Lakes located just beyond the newly reclaimed Buckingham canal.

Foscote Lakes

Foscote, MK16 6AH
Distance from campus: 4.5km
Newly restored by the same local hortacultural group who reinvigorated the Buckingham canal, these picturesque lakes have been given a new lease of life.
Situated just shy of 5 kilometres from Hunter Street campus, these lakes boast breathtaking views of unspoiled nature at its finest.

An area of true, unspoiled natural beauty.

Annie Buckingham Canal Society

By Foot

From Hunter Street, head along Nelson Street, turning right down Elm Street. From there, cross the bridge by the woolpack and head down Bourton Road to Bourton Park.
At the edge of the park near the basketball courts, head under the bridge and follow the bridal path for several hundred yards along the old canal.
